About Us

We’re a group of Computer Science students from Atlanta who are passionate about backend development and have a deep love for food. Our journey started with a simple curiosity about how technology works, but it quickly evolved into a strong desire to explore its limitless possibilities. With a special focus on combining our technical skills with our love for culinary arts, we set out to build a platform that brings both of our passions together. Our goal was to create a space where food enthusiasts can easily access a wealth of resources, including recipes, nutritional information, and cooking insights. We’ve spent countless hours coding, testing, and refining this platform, ensuring that it’s not only functional but also user-friendly and enjoyable for everyone who uses it. But our work goes beyond just the technical aspects. We’re united by a shared vision: to use our skills and knowledge to make a meaningful impact through technology. Our mission is to make a positive difference, one line of code and one recipe at a time.
